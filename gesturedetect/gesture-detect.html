Unreal VR Proof of Concept

A virtual reality system with head gestures and voice recognition.

Program Features

Head Gesture Detection

Users can nod to teleport, perform sharp turns left and right to rotate, and make quick movements forward and backward for interactions.

Voice Recognition

Implemented voice commands for additional interactions, making the experience more immersive and hands-free.

Teleportation System

Users gaze at a location to spawn a teleportation circle, then nod to teleport instantly.

Object Interaction

A forward sharp movement sends a ray trace to pick up objects, while a backward sharp movement throws the object.

How to Use

Teleportation

Look at the target location to spawn a teleportation circle, then nod to teleport.

Turning

Perform sharp head movements left or right to turn in the corresponding direction.

Picking Up Objects

Make a quick forward movement with your head to cast a ray trace and pick up an object.

Throwing Objects

Make a quick backward movement with your head to release the object and throw it.
